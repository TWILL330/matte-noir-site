Travon Williams

Founder & Chief Creative Strategist

Travon Williams is a visionary marketer, brand architect, and cultural strategist with over 15 years of experience leading paid media, digital campaigns, and performance-driven storytelling for global brands. He is the founder of Matte Noir, a luxury marketing agency engineered to amplify culture-first, data-smart brands through intentional design and strategic execution.

Travon's work sits at the intersection of psychology, culture, and technology. He has managed multi-million-dollar advertising portfolios across healthcare, tech, fashion, and music, delivering scalable results while preserving brand soul. As a cancer survivor and former collegiate vocalist, his creative approach is both analytical and deeply human—shaped by resilience, beauty, and clarity of vision.

Travon holds a Bachelor of Science in Biology (Pre-Med) from Mississippi State University, an ALB in Psychology from Harvard University, and is currently completing his MLA in Behavioral Psychology at Johns Hopkins University, where his research focuses on the cognitive and emotional effects of marketing and media on consumer behavior.
